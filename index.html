The Story

You take the role of a nighttime security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, a family restaurant similar to Chuck E. Cheese's. Your job is to monitor the establishment through security cameras from midnight to 6 AM.

The animatronic characters - Freddy Fazbear, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy - are allowed to walk around during the night to prevent their servos from locking up. However, they've been acting strangely...

According to phone calls from your predecessor, there was an "incident" involving the animatronics mistaking humans for endoskeletons without their costumes on. They would try to "stuff" the person into a spare Freddy Fazbear suit.

The Animatronics

Freddy Fazbear

The main attraction and leader of the band. Freddy becomes more active in later nights, often appearing in the dark when you least expect him.

Behavior: Moves slowly but strategically. Often appears in the dining area before moving to your office.

Bonnie the Bunny

The blue/purple rabbit animatronic who plays guitar. Bonnie is often the first to become active and the most aggressive.

Behavior: Moves quickly through the left side of the restaurant. Will stare at you from the doorway before attacking.

Chica the Chicken

The yellow chicken animatronic who holds a cupcake. Chica is often seen in the kitchen area.

Behavior: Moves through the right side of the restaurant. Known for appearing in the doorway suddenly.

Foxy the Pirate Fox

The out-of-order animatronic in Pirate Cove. Foxy runs down the hallway when not monitored.

Behavior: Requires constant monitoring. If ignored for too long, he will sprint down the hallway to your office.

Golden Freddy

The mysterious golden version of Freddy that appears as a rare hallucination. This ghostly animatronic doesn't follow normal movement patterns.

Behavior:

  • Appears when viewing the "East Hall Corner" camera (CAM 2B)
  • Can appear in your office if you check the camera at the wrong time
  • Immediately crashes your game if he fully manifests
  • Only appears on later nights (Night 3 onward)

Fun Fact: Golden Freddy may be connected to the "Missing Children Incident" mentioned in the newspaper clippings.

Gameplay Mechanics

Five Nights at Freddy's 1 features simple but tense gameplay mechanics:

  • Power Management: You have limited power each night (starting at 100%). Cameras, lights, and doors drain power.
  • Security Cameras: Use cameras to track animatronic movement. Some areas are more important than others.
  • Doors: Can be closed to block animatronics, but drain power quickly.
  • Lights: Check the hallways outside your doors to see if animatronics are present.
  • Audio Cues: Listen for footsteps, banging, or other sounds that indicate animatronic movement.
  • Jumpscares: If an animatronic manages to enter your office during the night, it will trap you with a jumpscare and you will lose and have to start the night from the beginning.

GAME BUGS & GLITCHES

Frozen Game Bug:

  • Occurs at ~1% battery power
  • Trigger by ALT+TAB to minimize game
  • Game becomes unresponsive but you can't die
  • Must minimize game again to fix

RARE ANIMATRONIC BEHAVIORS

Staring Into Camera:

  • All animatronics look at camera simultaneously
  • Extremely rare chance (less than 1%)
  • Happens just before they leave the stage

Bonnie's Scary Eyes:

  • Bonnie stares directly into camera
  • Eyes appear more intense than normal
